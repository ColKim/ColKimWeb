Recipe for Snickerdoodles

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 tsp of cream of tartar
  • 1/2 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/8 tsp of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cup of flour
  • 1/8 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon

Directions

  1. Cream butter until light and fluffy. Mix sugar, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt.
    Add the sugar mixture to the butter, and then egg and vanilla.
    Beat in as much of the flour as possible.
  2. Cover and chill for about 1 hour or until easy to handle.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Combine sugar and cinnamon in a bowl.
  4. Weigh dough into 15 gram size balls. Roll in sugar mix, and place 2 inches apart on pan.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes.
